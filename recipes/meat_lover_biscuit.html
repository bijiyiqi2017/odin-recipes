Meat Lover's Biscuit

Description

Meat Lover's Biscuit is a hearty, savory breakfast or brunch option that combines crispy bacon, savory sausage, and gooey cheese all stuffed inside a fluffy biscuit. It's the perfect comfort food for a weekend morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease a baking sheet.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in the butter using a pastry cutter or fork until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually add the milk, stirring until a dough forms.
  5. Fold in the sausage, bacon, and shredded cheese until well combined.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and roll it out to about 1-inch thick. Cut into rounds using a biscuit cutter or glass.
  7. Place biscuits on the prepared baking sheet, brush with a beaten egg, and bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
